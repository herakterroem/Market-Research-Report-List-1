<p><p>The foreign body detectors market includes three main types: magnetic separators, metal detectors, and X-ray machines. Magnetic separators identify and remove ferrous and non-ferrous metal contaminants from products, ensuring safety and quality in food and manufacturing. Metal detectors utilize electromagnetic fields to detect metallic objects in various materials, enhancing product integrity. X-ray machines provide advanced imaging to detect non-metallic foreign bodies, such as glass, plastic, and bone fragments, ensuring comprehensive inspection across diverse industries, including food processing and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
