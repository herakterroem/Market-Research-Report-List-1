<p><p>The multi-spectrum flame detectors market comprises three main types: UV flame detectors, IR flame detectors, and UV & IR flame detectors. UV flame detectors utilize ultraviolet radiation to identify flames, making them suitable for rapid detection in environments with minimal ambient light. IR flame detectors, on the other hand, sense infrared radiation emitted by flames, proving effective in bright conditions. UV & IR flame detectors combine both technologies, offering enhanced reliability and coverage, suitable for various industrial applications where fire safety is critical.</p></p>
