<p><p>The automotive parts cleaners and degreasers market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based products. Water-based cleaners utilize a mixture of water and biodegradable detergents, making them environmentally friendly and less toxic. They are ideal for light to moderate cleaning tasks. In contrast, solvent-based cleaners use organic solvents to dissolve grease and grime, offering powerful cleaning capabilities for heavy-duty applications. They tend to be more effective on stubborn contaminants but may pose environmental and health risks. Both types serve distinct needs in automotive maintenance.</p></p>
