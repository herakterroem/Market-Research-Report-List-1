<p><p>The laminate plastic film market consists of various types tailored for specific applications. Standard thermal laminating films are versatile and widely used for protection and presentation. Low-temperature thermal laminating films operate effectively at reduced heat, ideal for sensitive materials. Heat set laminating films involve a post-lamination heating process for enhanced durability. Pressure-sensitive films adhere without heat, offering convenience. Liquid laminates provide a fluid application method, enabling seamless coverage and flexibility in various printing and finishing processes.</p></p>
