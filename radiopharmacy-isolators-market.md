<p><p>Radiopharmacy isolators are specialized enclosures used for the safe handling of radioactive materials in the pharmaceutical industry. The market for these isolators is divided into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic isolators feature fully automated systems that require minimal manual intervention, enhancing safety and efficiency. In contrast, semi-automatic isolators combine automation with some manual operation, allowing for flexibility in handling processes while still offering a controlled environment. Both types aim to ensure the safety of personnel and integrity of radioactive compounds.</p></p>
