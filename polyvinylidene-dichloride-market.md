<p><p>The Polyvinylidene Dichloride (PVDC) market is categorized into two main types: resin and latex. PVDC resin is primarily used in films and coatings, offering excellent barrier properties against moisture, gases, and flavors, making it ideal for food packaging. On the other hand, PVDC latex is utilized in emulsions for various applications, including adhesives, paint, and sealants, providing superior adhesion and film-forming capabilities. Both types contribute significantly to industries like packaging, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
