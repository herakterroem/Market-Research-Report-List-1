<p><p>The veterinary immunodiagnostics market encompasses several types of diagnostic tests that utilize immune reactions to detect diseases in animals. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) are widely used for their sensitivity and specificity in identifying pathogens. Radioimmunoassays use radioactive isotopes for quantifying substances but are less common due to safety concerns. Rapid tests provide quick results and are often used in field diagnostics. Other market types may include western blotting and immunofluorescence, catering to diverse diagnostic needs in veterinary practices.</p></p>
