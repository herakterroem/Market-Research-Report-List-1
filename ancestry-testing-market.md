<p><strong>The Ancestry Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Y chromosome testing</li><li>Mitochondrial DNA testing</li><li>Single nucleotide polymorphism testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ancestry testing encompasses several market types focused on genetic analysis to trace lineage. Y chromosome testing targets paternal lineage, analyzing the Y chromosome passed from fathers to sons, revealing connections to male ancestors. Mitochondrial DNA testing examines maternal lineage through mitochondrial DNA inherited from mothers, illuminating maternal ancestry. Single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) testing analyzes variations in DNA sequences across the genome, providing insights into an individual's ethnic background and ancestral origins, making it a popular and comprehensive approach to ancestry research.</p></p>
