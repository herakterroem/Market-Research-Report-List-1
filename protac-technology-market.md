<p><p>PROTAC technology leverages two primary market types based on different E3 ligases: CRBN (Cereblon) and VHL (Von Hippel-Lindau). The CRBN-based market focuses on targeted protein degradation through ligands that recruit CRBN, facilitating the ubiquitination of specific proteins for degradation. Conversely, the VHL-based market utilizes VHL's unique ability to target hypoxic conditions and specific substrates for degradation. Both market types represent innovative approaches in drug discovery, providing avenues for addressing various diseases by eliminating unwanted proteins selectively.</p></p>
