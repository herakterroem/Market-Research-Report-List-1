<p><p>A Transformer Coupled Solid State Relay (TSSR) is an electronic switching device that uses an optically isolated transformer for control and isolation purposes to switch AC loads. Unlike traditional electromagnetic relays, TSSRs do not have moving parts, leading to increased reliability and reduced maintenance. They are commonly employed in industrial automation, power management, and HVAC systems due to their ability to handle high voltage and current with speed.</p><p>The Transformer Coupled Solid State Relay Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period.
