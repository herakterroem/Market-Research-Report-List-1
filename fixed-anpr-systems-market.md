<p><p>The Fixed Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) systems market comprises four key components: ANPR cameras, ANPR software, frame grabbers, and triggers. ANPR cameras capture high-resolution images of vehicle license plates, enabling accurate recognition. The software processes these images to extract and analyze plate information. Frame grabbers facilitate the transfer of image data to the software for further processing. Triggers, often sensors or loops, initiate the capture of images when vehicles approach, ensuring precise system operation and timely data collection.</p></p>
