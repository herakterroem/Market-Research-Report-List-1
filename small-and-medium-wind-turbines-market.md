<p><p>The small and medium wind turbines market comprises two main types: Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT) and Vertical-Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT). HAWTs are more common, featuring blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, optimized for high efficiency and larger energy capture in open spaces. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, offering advantages in urban settings due to their ability to capture wind from any direction and typically require less maintenance. Both types cater to diverse energy needs and applications.</p></p>
