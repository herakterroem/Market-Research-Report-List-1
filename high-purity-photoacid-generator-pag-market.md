<p><p>High Purity Photoacid Generators (PAGs) are specialized chemicals used in photolithography and other light-based processes to generate acids upon exposure to light. The market for PAGs includes photo-radical initiators and photo-cationic initiators, which differ in their mechanism of action. Photo-radical initiators produce free radicals upon light exposure, facilitating polymerization, while photo-cationic initiators generate cations that initiate cationic polymerization. Both types are essential in the development of advanced materials for electronics, coatings, and adhesives.</p></p>
