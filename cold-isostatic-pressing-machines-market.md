<p><strong>The Cold Isostatic Pressing Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Bag</li><li>Wet Bag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Isostatic Pressing (CIP) machines are primarily categorized into two types: dry bag and wet bag systems. Dry bag machines utilize a flexible, inflation-resistant bag filled with dry granules to apply uniform pressure on the material being compacted, typically used for ceramic and powder metallurgy applications. In contrast, wet bag machines employ a hydraulic fluid-filled bag, allowing for better conformity to intricate shapes and offering enhanced pressure distribution, often used for high-performance materials. Both types cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
