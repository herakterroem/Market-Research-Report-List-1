<p><p>The flea control market is divided into three key types: sprays, powders, and baits. Powders offer a longer-lasting solution, penetrating carpets and fabrics to kill fleas and their eggs over time. Baits, often combined with insect growth regulators, attract and eliminate fleas through ingestion.
