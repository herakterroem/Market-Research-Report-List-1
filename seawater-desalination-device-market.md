<p><strong>The Seawater Desalination Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis System</li><li>Distillation System</li><li>Reverse Osmosis Combined With Distillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seawater desalination device market encompasses three primary types: Reverse Osmosis (RO) Systems, Distillation Systems, and combined systems. RO Systems utilize semi-permeable membranes to filter out salt and impurities from seawater, making them energy-efficient and widely adopted. Distillation Systems, on the other hand, involve heating seawater to create steam, which is then condensed back into liquid, effectively removing salts. Combined systems integrate both RO and distillation methods to optimize efficiency and water quality, catering to various industrial and municipal needs.</p></p>
