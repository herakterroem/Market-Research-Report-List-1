<p><p>The pool alarm system market comprises several key types designed to enhance safety around swimming pools. Fence or wall-mounted pool alarms are installed at perimeters to detect unauthorized access. Cell-mounted infrared detectors utilize infrared technology to sense movement and trigger alerts when someone enters the pool area. Underwater motion alerts monitor the water surface for disturbances, providing immediate warnings when someone or something enters the pool unexpectedly. Together, these systems help ensure safety and prevent drowning incidents.</p></p>
