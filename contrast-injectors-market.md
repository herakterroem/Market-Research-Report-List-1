<p><p>Contrast injectors are specialized devices used to deliver contrast agents for medical imaging. The market is segmented into three main types: orally, rectally, and intravenously. Oral contrast injectors provide imaging agents through the gastrointestinal tract, enhancing visibility during scans like CT and MRI. Rectal injectors deliver agents for evaluating pelvic organs, particularly in colorectal imaging. Intravenous injectors, the most common, administer contrast agents directly into the bloodstream, ensuring rapid distribution for effective visualization of vascular structures and organs.</p></p>
