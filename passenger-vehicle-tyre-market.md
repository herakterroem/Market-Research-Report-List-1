<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle tyre market primarily consists of radial and bias tyres, each serving distinct performance needs. Radial tyres feature tensile belts that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering improved fuel efficiency, better grip, and enhanced durability, making them the preferred choice for modern vehicles. In contrast, bias tyres have plies that cross each other, providing a robust construction suited for off-road or heavy-duty vehicles, but generally offering less flexibility and fuel efficiency compared to radial tyres.</p></p>
