<p><p>The SMT SPI (Solder Paste Inspection) equipment market is categorized into inline and offline types. Inline SPI systems are integrated into the production line, offering real-time inspection of solder paste application to enhance efficiency and reduce defects during assembly. Offline SPI systems, contrastingly, operate separately from the production line, allowing for batch inspections and detailed analysis of printed circuit boards (PCBs) post-manufacturing. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring quality and reliability in electronic manufacturing processes.</p></p>
