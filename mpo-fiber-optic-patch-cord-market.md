<p><p>The MPO fiber optic patch cord market is predominantly segmented into two types: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode patch cords utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. In contrast, multimode patch cords support multiple light paths, allowing higher bandwidth over shorter distances, making them suitable for data centers and local area networks. Each type serves specific applications based on distance, bandwidth requirements, and overall network architecture.</p></p>
