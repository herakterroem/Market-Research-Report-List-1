<p><p>The medical micro molding market encompasses various materials tailored for the production of small-scale medical components. PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) is known for its strength and biocompatibility, making it ideal for implants. Bioabsorbables are materials that safely dissolve in the body, often used for sutures or drug delivery. Silicone offers flexibility and durability, frequently utilized in devices like catheters. Rubber, valued for its elasticity, is commonly found in seals and gaskets. Other materials include polymers and metals, addressing diverse medical applications.</p></p>
