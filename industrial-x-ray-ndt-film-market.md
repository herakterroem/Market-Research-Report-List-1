<p><p>The Industrial X-Ray NDT Film market comprises two main types: Screen Type Films and Non-Screen Type Films. Screen Type Films are designed to work with intensifying screens, which enhance image quality and reduce exposure times. These films yield higher sensitivity and sharper images, making them suitable for various inspection applications. In contrast, Non-Screen Type Films do not require screens, offering direct exposure to X-rays. They are preferred in scenarios needing high detail and resolution, though they may require longer exposure times.</p></p>
