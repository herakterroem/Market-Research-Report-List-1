<p><strong>The Ceramic Hip Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ceramics Femoral Head</li><li>Ceramics Lining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic hip prosthesis market is divided into two main types: ceramic femoral heads and ceramic linings. Ceramic femoral heads are designed to fit onto the stem of the hip implant, providing a smooth, durable surface that reduces wear and tear, ultimately enhancing the longevity of the prosthesis. Ceramic linings, on the other hand, are used within the acetabular component of the hip joint, offering superior wear resistance and biocompatibility, which helps to minimize friction and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
