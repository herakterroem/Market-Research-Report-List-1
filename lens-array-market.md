<p><p>The lens array market is categorized into one-dimensional and two-dimensional types based on their configuration and application. One-dimensional lens arrays consist of a single row of lenses, ideal for applications like optical sensors and display systems where linear light manipulation is needed. In contrast, two-dimensional lens arrays feature a grid arrangement, offering enhanced versatility in light distribution and focusing, suitable for advanced imaging systems and spatial light modulation. Both types cater to various industries, including telecommunications and consumer electronics.</p></p>
