<p><p>The wireless flow sensor market is segmented into several types based on communication protocols. Zigbee flow sensors operate on low power and are ideal for mesh network applications, enhancing scalability in smart systems. Wi-Fi flow sensors leverage existing Wi-Fi networks for broader connectivity, enabling remote monitoring. Other wireless flow sensors may include technologies like LoRaWAN or NFC, catering to specific applications and industry needs.</p></p>
