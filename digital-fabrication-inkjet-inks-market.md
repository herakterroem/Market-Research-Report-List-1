<p><p>The Digital Fabrication Inkjet Inks market is primarily segmented into two types: dye-based and pigment-based inks. Dye-based inks offer vibrant colors and are best suited for applications requiring high-quality imaging on absorbent substrates, such as textiles and paper. In contrast, pigment-based inks provide greater durability and resistance to fading, making them ideal for outdoor applications and long-lasting prints on various media. Both types cater to distinct demands in the digital printing landscape, influencing their respective market dynamics.</p></p>
