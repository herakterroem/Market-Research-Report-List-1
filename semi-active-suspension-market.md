<p><ul><li>Switchable Damping Suspension</li><li>Continuous Adjustable Damping Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-active suspension market features two main types: switchable damping suspension and continuous adjustable damping suspension. Switchable damping systems provide predefined settings that can be toggled based on driving conditions, offering improved comfort and handling. In contrast, continuous adjustable damping systems allow for real-time adjustments of the damper settings, responding seamlessly to road conditions and driver inputs. Both types enhance vehicle stability and ride quality, contributing to a safer and more enjoyable driving experience.</p></p>
