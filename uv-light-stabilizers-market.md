<p><ul><li>UV Absorbers</li><li>HALS</li><li>Benzoates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV light stabilizers market comprises three main types: UV absorbers, hindered amine light stabilizers (HALS), and benzoates. UV absorbers function by absorbing harmful UV radiation and converting it into heat, thereby preventing substrate degradation. HALS are effective in capturing free radicals generated by UV exposure, significantly extending the lifespan of materials. Benzoates serve as effective UV blockers that protect polymers by preventing color fading and mechanical property loss. Together, these stabilizers enhance the durability of various products against UV damage.</p></p>
