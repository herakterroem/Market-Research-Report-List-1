<p><p>2.5D and 3D Through-Silicon Via (TSV) technologies represent advanced packaging methods that enable higher performance, tighter integration, and miniaturization of semiconductor devices. Conversely, 3D TSV stacks chips vertically, allowing for more compact designs and further improvement in performance and power efficiency.</p><p>The 2.5D and 3D TSV market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period. Key trends include a shift towards heterogeneous integration, where different semiconductor technologies are combined on a single platform, and the growing focus on sustainable semiconductor manufacturing practices.</p><p>Emerging applications in sectors such as automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics are boosting the need for innovative packaging solutions, enabling manufacturers to optimize performance while addressing size constraints.
