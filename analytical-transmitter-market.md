<p><p>The analytical transmitter market comprises two main types: multi-parameter and single-parameter transmitters. Multi-parameter transmitters can measure and transmit multiple analytical parameters simultaneously, making them ideal for complex applications that require real-time monitoring of various conditions, such as water quality or industrial processes. In contrast, single-parameter transmitters are designed to monitor and transmit one specific measurement, such as pH or conductivity. Both types serve distinct applications, with multi-parameter transmitters offering versatility and single-parameter transmitters focusing on precision.</p></p>
