<p><ul><li>Condensing Rotary Regenerative Heat Exchangers</li><li>Sorption Rotary Regenerative Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary regenerative heat exchangers market is divided into two main types: condensing and sorption. Condensing rotary regenerative heat exchangers transfer heat from exhaust gases to incoming air, promoting energy efficiency and reducing operational costs in industrial processes. Sorption rotary regenerative heat exchangers utilize adsorption or absorption principles to capture and reuse heat, optimizing thermal management. Both types enhance energy recovery and sustainability, contributing to greener technologies in various sectors, including HVAC and power generation.</p></p>
