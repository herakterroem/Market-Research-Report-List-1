<p><ul><li>Load-lock type</li><li>Batch Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-chamber sputtering system market features two primary types: load-lock type and batch type. Load-lock systems streamline the process by enabling quick sample transfers without breaking the vacuum, enhancing throughput and efficiency for high-volume production. In contrast, batch-type systems process multiple substrates simultaneously within a single chamber, optimizing resource use for smaller production runs. Both types cater to diverse applications in semiconductor manufacturing and materials research, facilitating advanced thin-film deposition and improving overall operational productivity.</p></p>
