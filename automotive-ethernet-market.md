<p><ul><li>Automotive Local Area Network (LAN)</li><li>Automotive Metropolitan Area Network (MAN)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Ethernet market is divided into two primary types: Automotive Local Area Network (LAN) and Automotive Metropolitan Area Network (MAN). Automotive LAN focuses on short-range communication within vehicles, enabling features like advanced driver-assistance systems and in-vehicle infotainment. In contrast, Automotive MAN covers broader connectivity, linking multiple vehicles or large fleets, facilitating real-time data exchange, and enhancing vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication. Both types support the growing demand for smart, connected automotive technologies and improved safety features.</p></p>
