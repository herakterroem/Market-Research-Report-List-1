<p><p>The nuclear medicine equipment market comprises primarily two types: Hybrid SPECT and Standalone SPECT. Hybrid SPECT combines single-photon emission computed tomography with other imaging modalities, such as CT or MRI, enabling comprehensive, high-resolution imaging that improves diagnostic capabilities. In contrast, Standalone SPECT systems focus solely on nuclear imaging, providing targeted functional information about organs and tissues. Both types play crucial roles in disease diagnosis, treatment monitoring, and research, catering to diverse clinical requirements and enhancing patient care.</p></p>
