<p><strong>The Dual Axis Solar Tracker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polar-axis Tracking</li><li>Altitude–azimuth Tracking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual-axis solar tracker market is primarily divided into two types: polar-axis tracking and altitude–azimuth tracking. Polar-axis trackers rotate around a horizontal axis aligned with Earth's rotation, optimizing solar energy capture throughout the day. Altitude–azimuth trackers control panels’ angles in both vertical and horizontal planes, allowing for maximum sunlight exposure regardless of seasonal changes. Both types enhance energy efficiency and output, making them popular choices for solar power plants and large-scale energy projects.</p></p>
