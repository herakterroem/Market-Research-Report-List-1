<p><p>CT (Computed Tomography) and PET (Positron Emission Tomography) scanners are advanced imaging technologies utilized primarily in medical diagnostics. CT scanners use X-ray data collected from various angles to produce cross-sectional images of bones, blood vessels, and soft tissues, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions. PET scanners, on the other hand, detect radiation emitted from radioactive tracers injected into the patient, providing insights into metabolic processes and cellular functions.</p><p>The CT and PET Scanners Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, advancements in imaging technology, and the rising demand for early diagnostic methods. Additionally, growing investments in healthcare infrastructure and a surge in the geriatric population are contributing to this market expansion.
