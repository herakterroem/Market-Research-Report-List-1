<p><p>Blown film extrusion lines produce plastic films by melting resin and extruding it through a die to form a tube. The market is segmented into 3-layer, 5-layer, and 7-layer systems, each offering different performance characteristics. 3-layer lines are cost-effective and suitable for basic films. 5-layer lines provide enhanced barrier properties and strength, ideal for packaging applications. 7-layer lines deliver superior performance with customized properties, catering to specific industry needs, such as food packaging and advanced protective films.</p></p>
