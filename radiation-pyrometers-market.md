<p><ul><li>Reflective Type</li><li>Refraction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation pyrometers are specialized devices used to measure temperature from a distance by detecting infrared radiation emitted from an object. The market is primarily divided into two types: reflective types, which analyze radiation reflected off surfaces to gauge temperature, making them suitable for shiny or high-emissivity materials; and refraction types, which utilize the bending of light to measure temperature, often employed in applications requiring precise, non-contact measurements. Both types are critical in industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
