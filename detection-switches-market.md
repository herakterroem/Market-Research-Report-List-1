<p><p>Detection switches are essential components used in various applications for monitoring and control purposes. The market includes SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) switches, which allow connections to two different circuits, providing versatility in detecting states. SPST-NC (Single Pole Single Throw - Normally Closed) switches are designed to remain closed under normal conditions, only opening when triggered. These types are crucial for safety and functionality in systems such as alarms, industrial machinery, and automation controls, where reliable detection is vital.</p></p>
