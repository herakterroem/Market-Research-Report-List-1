<p><p>The wastewater treatment market includes various filtration methods, notably granular media filtration and fiber filtration. Granular media filtration employs layers of sand, gravel, or other materials to remove contaminants through physical and biological processes, making it effective for large particles and suspended solids. In contrast, fiber filtration utilizes fine, fibrous materials to capture smaller particles and microorganisms, enhancing effluent quality. Both methods are crucial for improving water quality and comply with environmental regulations, ensuring safe discharge or reuse.</p></p>
