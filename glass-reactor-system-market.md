<p><p>The glass reactor system market comprises primarily two types: single jacketed and double jacketed reactor systems. Single jacketed reactors have an outer jacket that allows for heating or cooling, suitable for simple applications. In contrast, double jacketed reactors feature two layers of glass, providing enhanced temperature control and safety, making them ideal for sensitive chemical reactions. Both systems are widely used in pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and research labs, offering efficient and transparent solutions for process monitoring and control.</p></p>
