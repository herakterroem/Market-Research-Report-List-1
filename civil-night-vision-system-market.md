<p><p>The Civil Night Vision System market comprises two primary types: Low Light Night Vision Systems and Infrared Night Vision Systems. Low Light Night Vision Systems amplify available ambient light, enabling visibility in dark conditions, crucial for activities like wildlife observation and security. On the other hand, Infrared Night Vision Systems detect heat signatures and create images based on thermal radiation, making them ideal for search and rescue operations, surveillance, and military applications. Both technologies enhance visibility during nighttime or challenging lighting conditions.</p></p>
